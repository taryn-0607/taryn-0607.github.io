Art by Taryn

Hi, I am Taryn, a digital art student exploring character design and interactive media.

My latest project features a quirky purple blob brought to life in Unity from initial sketches to full movement. I enabled him to walk, run, jump, fall and die just as he would in a game.

This site is a space to share my process, experiments, and love for turning 2D art into playable characters.

You can follow me and check out my work on my instagram @tar_art_07.
